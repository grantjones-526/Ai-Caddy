# Ai Caddy - Live Demo Script (90 seconds)

## Introduction (10 seconds)
"Hi, I'm Grant, and I'm excited to show you Ai Caddy - an AI-powered golf club recommendation system that helps golfers make smarter club selections on the course."

---

## The Problem (15 seconds)
"Every golfer faces the same challenge: standing over a shot, trying to decide which club to use. Do you go with your 7 iron or 8 iron? How does the lie affect your distance? Traditional methods rely on guesswork and memory, leading to inconsistent performance."

---

## The Solution & Main Features (45 seconds)
"Ai Caddy solves this by using machine learning to analyze your historical shot data and provide personalized club recommendations.

**Key Features:**

1. **Smart Recommendations** - The KNN algorithm analyzes your past shots, considering distance, lie, hole bend, and shot shape to recommend the best club for your current situation.

2. **Performance Tracking** - Track every round and shot, with detailed statistics showing your average distance for each club from different lies - fairway, rough, and tee box.

3. **GPS Shot Tracking** - Use your phone's GPS to automatically measure shot distances on the course, making data entry seamless.

4. **Launch Monitor Integration** - Import data from popular devices like Garmin R10, SkyTrak+, and Flightscope Mevo+.

5. **Visual Analytics** - Interactive visualizations show how your shots cluster in feature space, helping you understand your game better."

---

## Tech Stack (10 seconds)
"Ai Caddy is built with Django and Python, using scikit-learn's K-Nearest Neighbors algorithm for recommendations. The frontend features a modern, responsive design with Plotly.js for data visualization."
